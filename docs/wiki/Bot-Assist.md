# Bot Assist

Bot Assist is an AI companion that helps you play by tapping safe cells automatically.

## How It Works

- Activated by pressing a key or tapping the bot button
- Costs Dust currency to activate and maintain
- The AI identifies safe (non-purple) cells and taps them for you
- Bot accuracy and speed depend on how much Dust you spend

## Strategy Tips

- Use Bot Assist during boss events when the grid is confusing
- Save Dust for critical moments — don't waste it early
- Bot Assist doesn't guarantee survival — purple cells can still appear where the bot taps
- Combine with Freeze or Shield for maximum survival time

## Earning Dust

- Complete games (longer survival = more Dust)
- Unlock achievements
- Daily check-in rewards
- Daily challenges (e.g., "Score 50", "Survive 60s")
- Weekly challenges (e.g., "Play 10 games")
