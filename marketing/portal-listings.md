## 1. itch.io

**Title:** Don't Touch Purple

**Tagline:** Tap fast. Dodge chaos. One more try.

**Short Description:**
A reflex-based grid-tapping game where every second counts. Tap safe cells, dodge purple, and survive bomb surges, inversion storms, and 13 types of chaos cells. Features 15 animated WebGL backgrounds, 4 game modes, 37 achievements, and global leaderboards. Free. No ads. No pay-to-win. Just pure "one more try" addiction.

**Long Description:**
Don't Touch Purple is a fast-paced reflex game built for players who crave that "just one more run" feeling.

The rules are simple: tap the safe cells, avoid purple. But every 30 seconds, boss events throw the grid into chaos — Bomb Surge floods the board with explosives, Inversion Storm flips your controls. You'll face shields, freezes, multipliers, slides, invisibility, corruption, duplication portals, time slows, mirrors, and split screens.

Four game modes keep it fresh: Classic for pure reflexes, Evolve for progressive difficulty, Practice for warmups, and a Daily Challenge that resets every 24 hours.

Unlock 37 achievements. Earn dust. Climb the global and weekly leaderboards. Choose from 15 animated WebGL backgrounds — Galaxy, Hyperspeed, Silk, Lightning, and more.

Built with React 19, TypeScript, and WebGL. Mobile-first. Works on desktop with keyboard. Free to play, forever.

**Tags:** reflex, arcade, tapping, puzzle, reaction, speed, casual, challenge, leaderboard, webgl

**Genre:** Action / Reflex

**Platforms:** Web (HTML5)
